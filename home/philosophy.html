Philosophy

"Working towards ninja levels of physical longevity."

Being able to move your body with pain free ease and strength is crucial, this means a big focus on mobility to create a highly resilient body.


A body that is athletic and elegant. I strive for training to be fun and a constant learning experience of bodily awareness. The joy of being able to throw myself into various activities from wake boarding to playing frisbee to pole fitness is second to none!


Being fit and healthy is my life-long commitment to myself, its therapeutic and empowering all at the same time. This is what I aim to teach others, training for life, let's get strong and live long!
