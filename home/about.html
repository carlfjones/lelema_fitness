About Me

"Having not grown up as a particularly sporty or active kid, becoming a fitness and movement coach was completely unpredictable."

I had a very loose interest in dance and gymnastics but let me tell you I never excelled. I remember prancing around in hand-me-down tutu's as a toddler on the sofa and joining a dance club in high school, which lets face it was really just a social club! I did gymnastics in primary school and the most impressive thing I could do was kick out of a wheel...with a few mattresses stacked under my feet.

Fast forward into my early 20's, post uni. I was rushing to meet some friends in Leicester square so I walked up the escalator as fast as I could...I tried...but my lungs made me pause.

That was the beginning of my fitness journey and it was onwards and upwards from there.

At the start I began working out at home, and I swore I would never go to a gym because I'd always heard they were bacteria city. I found the at home Insanity workouts and began my obsession with wanting a 6 pack like Shaun T promised.

It's been quite a journey, from wanting to be Miss Skinny 6 pack abs to

"ARNIE IS FITNESS"

I could lift some real heavy weights after my first year of serious weight training but it came at a cost. I was burning the candle at both ends and my back didn't hold up, but I looked great and was strong AF. So eventually my endless desire to understand the human body, especially my own has led me to today. I've learnt so much from my own injuries and experiences which has made me into the competent coach that I am proud to be now.

Exploring all the ways in which the human body can move, with control, without injury, to feel good and strong is the path I'm on right now.
