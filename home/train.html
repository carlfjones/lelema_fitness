Move well with Lele

What is the point of looking good if you only wake up with pain everyday? If you move well and train for physical longevity you will be able to perform exercises and activities that bring you joy, this can only mean that you will feel good and as a result, look good. If this sounds like what you need, I can help you reach your destination. There are a few options below that you can sign up for separately or in a combination:

1-1 Face to face

I am currently London based. Face to face is my favourite way to train clients as its the best way for me to assess how you move from all angles.

1-1 Online

Modern technology means I can train you anywhere in the world!!

Small group training - Online

I really enjoy group training as it means I can teach the importance of moving well and moving often to several humans at once!
